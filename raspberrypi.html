Rasberry Pi

According to OpenSource.com, Rasberry Pi is created by Rasberry Pi foundation. The device is serires of single-board computers. It was launched and publicly released in 2012. With Raspberry Pi it allows programmers/developers to construct regular and miniature desktop computer. Likewise, it is also commonly used for IoT devices. The latest Rasberry Pi is the Raspberry Pi Zero 2 W.
